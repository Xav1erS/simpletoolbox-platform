Typography

Readable hierarchy with compact density

Page H1: primary page identity

Section H2: major content block

Card group H3: secondary grouping

Card H4: standard card title

Lead text is reserved for short page intros and explanatory blurbs.

Body text is the default layer for help text, tool descriptions, and result explanations.

Caption is used for metadata, counters, field notes, and secondary helpers.
