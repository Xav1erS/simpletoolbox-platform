Privacy Policy Your Privacy Matters

At Simple Toolbox ("we", "us", or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our online tools at simpletoolbox.dev.

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

Information Collection

We do not require you to register or provide personal information to use our tools. However, we may collect the following data:

  • Usage Data: We use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how our site is used. This may include your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.
  • Tool Input: The data you enter into our tools (such as text, code, or files) is processed temporarily to provide the requested service.

Use of Data

  • Service Provision: The data you input into our tools is processed in real-time to provide the output or conversion you requested.
  • Analytics: Usage data helps us understand traffic patterns and improve our site's performance and user experience.
  • Security: We may use your IP address to monitor for and prevent malicious activity or attacks.

Data Retention and Security

  • Temporary Processing: The data you enter into our tools is not stored on our servers after the processing is complete. It is held in memory only for the duration of your session.
  • No Personal Storage: We do not store personal information (such as names or email addresses) unless explicitly provided by you (for example, in a support email).
  • Security Measures: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access or disclosure of the data we process.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect usage information. Google may use this data to contextualize ads on other websites. You can opt out of Google Analytics here.
  • Google AdSense: Our site uses Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to personalize ads based on your visit. You can manage your ad preferences here.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@simpletoolbox.dev.
