Frequently Asked Questions

Is my image data secure?
Yes! All image processing happens entirely within your browser. Your photos never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.
What image formats are supported?
We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats for both input and output. You can also convert between these formats during resizing.
How many images can I process at once?
You can upload and process up to 50 images simultaneously. The tool supports batch processing with individual settings for each image.
What are the maximum image dimensions?
You can resize images up to 10,000×10,000 pixels. The tool will automatically maintain aspect ratio to prevent distortion.
Do you support social media template sizes?
Yes! We provide 20+ social media platform templates including Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, LinkedIn, and TikTok with their exact recommended dimensions.
Is there a file size limit?
We recommend keeping files under 50MB for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process but will still work.
