How to Use Image Background Remover

Our free image background remover is designed to be intuitive and powerful. Whether you need to process a single product photo or refine a complex portrait, this guide will walk you through every feature. All AI processing happens locally in your browser—your images never leave your device.

Quick Start: Remove a Background in Four Steps

1 Upload Your Image

Click the “Upload Image” button or drag and drop an image file (JPG, PNG, WebP up to 10 MB) into the designated area. The tool will load the image and immediately begin AI analysis.

2 Review the Automatic Result

Within seconds, the AI will generate a preliminary mask, removing the background and displaying the subject on a transparent background. Use the side‑by‑side slider to compare the original and result.

3 Refine the Mask (Optional)

If the automatic removal missed details or included unwanted areas, switch to the “Edit Mask” tab. Use the green brush to add foreground areas or the red brush to remove background areas. Adjust brush size with the slider.

4 Download the Result

Choose your output format:

  • PNG with transparency – The standard choice for designers.
  • Solid color background – Select white, black, or enter a custom hex color.

Click “Download PNG” to save the final image to your device.

Tip: For best results, use images with clear contrast between subject and background. Complex backgrounds (e.g., foliage, patterned walls) may require manual refinement with the brush tool.

Using the Editing Tools

The built‑in editing tools give you precise control over the final mask.

1 Zoom and Pan

Click the zoom buttons (or use your mouse wheel) to zoom in for detail work. Click and drag the image to pan. The “Reset View” button returns to the default zoom and position.

2 Undo and Redo

Made a mistake? Use the “Undo” button (or Ctrl+Z) to reverse your last brush stroke. “Redo” (Ctrl+Y) reapplies it. Up to 20 actions are stored.

3 Show/Hide Mask Overlay

Toggle the “Show Mask” checkbox to display a semi‑transparent green overlay over the foreground area. This helps you spot small errors.

Understanding Output Options

You can tailor the result to your specific needs.

  • Transparent PNG – Ideal for graphic design, web use, or placing the subject onto a new background in an image editor.
  • White Background – The standard for e‑commerce product photos; many marketplaces require pure‑white (#FFFFFF) backgrounds.
  • Black Background – Creates a dramatic, high‑contrast look suitable for social media banners or artistic compositions.
  • Custom Color – Enter any hex color (e.g., #165DFF) to match your brand palette or project theme.

Managing Your History

The tool keeps a local history of your last five processed images.

  • View – The history panel (right side on desktop, below on mobile) shows thumbnails of your recent images.
  • Reload – Click any thumbnail to reload that image into the editor, including its mask and editing state.
  • Clear – Use the “Clear History” button to delete all stored images. History is stored only in your current browser and device.

Privacy and Performance

Our remover is built with privacy as a core principle:

  • No Server Uploads – All AI processing runs locally using TensorFlow.js. Your images are never transmitted over the internet.
  • No Registration – Use the tool immediately without creating an account.
  • Fast Processing – The optimized model processes most images in 2‑5 seconds, depending on your device’s performance.

There are no daily limits. You can process as many images as you need, at full original quality.
