Privacy Policy Your Privacy Matters

At Simple Toolbox ("we", "us", or "our"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website simpletoolbox.dev.

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Simple Toolbox. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your data.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, as well as information automatically collected when you use our services:

Usage Data

We use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how our site is used. This may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited.

Tool Input

The data you enter into our tools (such as text, code, or files) is processed temporarily to provide the requested service.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

Provide and maintain our services

The data you input into our tools is processed in real-time to provide the output or conversion you requested.

Improve and personalize your experience

Usage data helps us understand traffic patterns and improve our site's performance and user experience.

Ensure the security of our platform

We may use your IP address to monitor for and prevent malicious activity or attacks.

Comply with legal obligations

We may use your information to meet legal requirements.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We take the following measures to protect your data:

Temporary Processing

The data you enter into our tools is not stored on our servers after the processing is complete. It is held in memory only for the duration of your session.

No Personal Storage

We do not store personal information (such as names or email addresses) unless explicitly provided by you (for example, in a support email).

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access or disclosure of the data we process.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect usage information. Google may use this data to contextualize ads on other websites. You can opt out of Google Analytics here.

Google AdSense

Our site uses Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to personalize ads based on your visit. You can manage your ad preferences here.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
